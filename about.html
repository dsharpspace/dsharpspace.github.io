D#: 深圳科技与艺术驻留项目

该驻留项目提供一个合作环境，艺术家将与沉浸式设计小组 Immersive Design Group和声音研究科研组 Sound Studies Group合作，创作融合创意与先进多模态设计的开创性作品。

我们的使命

我们致力于：

  • 打破艺术与技术之间的界限，创造一个感官体验与人类触觉无缝融合的空间。
  • 开发一个创新平台，让技术成为情感共鸣艺术创作的合作伙伴。
  • 通过将以人为本的传统艺术与尖端技术结合，重新发现感官的丰富性。

主办方：

  • 浸式设计小组 Immersive Design Group
  • 声音研究科研组 Sound Studies Group

D#: Shenzhen Tech + Art Residency Program

This residency offers a collaborative environment where artists work alongside the Immersive Design Group and Sound Studies Group to create pioneering works that blend creativity with advanced multimodal design.

Our Mission

We aim to:

  • Break down boundaries between art and technology, creating spaces where sensory experiences and human touch merge seamlessly.
  • Develop an innovative platform where technology becomes a collaborator in crafting emotionally resonant art.
  • Rediscover sensory richness by merging human-centered artistic practices with the possibilities of cutting-edge technologies.

Organizers

  • Immersive Design Group
  • Sound Studies Group
