2025年的主题

感官联觉：融合技术与艺术，探索多维体验

我们的愿景

该项目邀请艺术家、设计师和技术专家探索艺术与技术如何结合，创造沉浸式、多感官的体验。通过融合人本艺术理念与先进技术工具，我们的目标是为感官互动开辟新的视野，并建立一个以人类感官与技术和谐共存为基础的创意生态系统。

项目目标

建立创意平台

  • 为艺术家提供空间和资源，以实验多模态设计和创意表达。
  • 支持通过传感器、灯光、触觉技术和音响系统等互动技术，融合多种艺术作品或装置，创造多感官体验。

研究技术与感官艺术的融合

  • 探索将多模态设计融入艺术实践的方法论。
  • 鼓励技术专家与艺术家之间的合作，激发创造力与创新。

教育与社区建设

  • 举办工作坊和研讨会，为创作者分享多模态设计的知识。
  • 推动跨学科学习，汇聚艺术、设计和技术领域的参与者。
  • 向当地社区展示艺术创作，突显多模态设计的价值。

提供参与式艺术体验

  • 策划沉浸式展览，让观众成为艺术作品的一部分，与之直接互动。
  • 通过动作、声音、生物信号等感官输入，启用互动体验。

建立可持续的创意生态系统

  • 为本地艺术家运营驻留项目，提供可持续发展的创意空间。
  • 制定方法以长期保存和维护创意作品。

项目结构

创意驻留计划

  • 参与者将获得技术设备、专属工作空间和专家指导支持。
  • 项目将融合多模态技术（如传感器、语音识别、触觉反馈）与多种艺术实践。
  • 参与者将在定期的工作分享会上展示他们的进展，并与导师和同行讨论他们的创作过程。

展览与展示

  • 参与者有机会向本地和国际社区展示他们的作品。
  • 沉浸式展览将允许观众参与并与艺术作品互动。

预期成果

创新创作

  • 多模态技术与多感官艺术实践的融合将产生新的艺术表达形式。
  • 艺术体验将更加多样化，重点关注感官交互。

扩展参与式艺术

  • 沉浸式互动体验将深化对艺术与技术关系的理解。
  • 观众将成为艺术作品的有机组成部分，提升艺术体验。

强化跨学科合作

  • 跨学科合作将激活创意生态系统，汇聚艺术家、设计师和工程师。

扩展艺术的社会角色

  • 驻留项目将鼓励重新审视感官体验在数字时代中的作用。
  • 邀请当地社区通过艺术体验技术，探索新的感官视角。

2025 Topic

Sensory Synesthesia: Fusing Technology and Art to Create Multisensory Experiences

Our Vision

This program invites artists, designers, and technologists to explore how art and technology can create immersive, multisensory experiences. By combining human-centered artistic sensibilities with advanced tools, our goal is to open new horizons for sensory interaction and establish a creative ecosystem rooted in the harmony between human senses and technology.

Program Goals

Building a Creative Platform

  • Provide space and resources for artists to experiment with multimodal design and creative expression.
  • Support creations that integrate diverse artworks or installations with multisensory experiences by incorporating interactive technologies such as sensors, lighting, haptics, and sound systems.

Researching the Integration of Technology and Sensory Art

  • Explore methodologies for blending multimodal design into artistic practice.
  • Encourage collaboration between technical experts and artists to foster creativity and innovation.

Education and Community Building

  • Host workshops and seminars to share knowledge of multimodal design for creators.
  • Promote cross-disciplinary learning, bringing together participants from art, design, and technology fields.
  • Showcase artistic creations to local communities, highlighting the value of multimodal design.

Providing Participatory Art Experiences

  • Plan immersive exhibitions that invite audiences to become part of the artwork, engaging directly with it.
  • Enable interactive experiences through movement, voice, biometric signals, and other sensory inputs.

Establishing a Sustainable Creative Ecosystem

  • Operate a residency program for local artists, providing a space for sustainable creative growth.
  • Develop methods to preserve and maintain creative works in the long term.

Program Structure

Creative Residency Program

  • Participants will have access to technical equipment, dedicated workspace, and mentorship from experts.
  • Projects will integrate multimodal technologies (e.g., sensors, voice recognition, tactile feedback) with various artistic practices.
  • Participants will present their progress during regular work-sharing meetings and discuss their processes with mentors and peers.

Exhibition and Presentation

  • Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their works to local and international communities.
  • Immersive exhibitions will allow audience participation and interaction with the artworks.

Expected Outcomes

Innovation in Creation

  • The fusion of multimodal technology with diverse sensory-focused artistic practices will generate new forms of artistic expression.
  • Artistic experiences will diversify, focusing on sensory interactions.

Expansion of Participatory Art

  • Immersive, interactive experiences will deepen the understanding of the relationship between art and technology.
  • The audience will become an integral part of the artwork, enhancing the artistic experience.

Strengthening Interdisciplinary Collaboration

  • Cross-disciplinary collaboration will activate a creative ecosystem, uniting artists, designers, and engineers.

Expansion of Art’s Social Role

  • The residency will encourage a re-examination of the role of sensory experience in the digital age.
  • Local communities will be invited to experience technology through art and explore new sensory perspectives.
